The Elder Dragons of Kaelthas

Long before the age of the Sundering, when the world was still warm with the breath of creation, the elder dragons carved their dominion from the living rock of the deep mountains…

Origins & Lineage

The first record of Vaeltharion the Unmade appears in the Codex Primordius, a fragmentary text discovered in the ruins of the Obsidian Library. Cross-referenced with the Draconic Annals, scholars place his emergence roughly 4,000 years before the current age.

Physical Description

Vaeltharion's scales shift between deep obsidian and iridescent violet depending on ambient temperature. Each scale measures roughly 18 inches across at the dorsal ridge.
